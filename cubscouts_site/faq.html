Frequently Asked Questions

Who can join Cub Scouts?

Cub Scouting is for boys and girls in grades K–5. Families are welcome at all events.

When and where do you meet?

Thursdays at 6:00 pm at United Methodist Church, 7822 E Epworth Forest Rd, North Webster.
